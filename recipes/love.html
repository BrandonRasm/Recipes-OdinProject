Love

Description

Everybody wants it. Nobody's got it. Love is what makes the world go round and by following this simple recipe you can find love or you're money back.

Ingredients:

  • 2 people
  • 1(or more) common interest(s)
  • Time. If your low on time you can substitute desperation at a 2-1 ratio.

Steps:

  1. Place both people at a low simmer at a dinner table
  2. Mince and sprinkle in common interests slowly
  3. Add time until desired results.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
