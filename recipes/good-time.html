Good Time Recipe

Recipe Description

Follow the steps listed below and you'll be sure to have a great night

Ingredients:

  • Your crew/posse/squad/gang
  • Some tunes(preferable bumpin')
  • Snacks and drinks
  • An activity(optional)

Steps:

  1. Set your tunes at a medium volume in a large room.
  2. Add all the snacks and drinks in a bunch to the side of the room.
  3. Slowly mix in all the members of your crew(or posse or gang).
  4. If crew members have trouble mixing consider adding an activity to encourage co-mingling
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
