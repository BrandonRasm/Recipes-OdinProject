Disaster Recipe

Recipe Description

Like alcohol and fireworks sometime mixing 2 fun things can end up having not so fun results. If you follow the steps down below you'll surely have a recipe for disaster.

Ingredients:

  • My friend Derek
  • My friend Devon
  • The keys to my 2001 honda civic

Steps:

  1. Leave Derek and Devon in a room unattended for 5 minutes.
  2. Reluctantly repond yes when they request your car keys.
  3. Wait 45 minutes for a phone call confirming that recipe has worked.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
